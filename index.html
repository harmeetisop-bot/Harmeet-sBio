01 — About

Who's behind the code

I'm Harmeet — a developer and creator who lives at the intersection of code and gaming. I've been working across Roblox, web development, and multiple gaming platforms, continuously building projects I'm genuinely proud of.

I work with Luau, Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS — enough to build whatever the project demands. On Roblox, I've built complex systems and scripts. Outside of Roblox, I focus on full-stack web development and creating engaging digital experiences across different platforms.

Gaming is more than a hobby for me — it's research and passion. I play Minecraft, Roblox, COD MW3, GTA V, COD Mobile, PUBG and more, which means I understand what makes interactive experiences feel right. I create content across YouTube, Instagram, Snapchat and Discord, sharing my journey as I go.

Let's connect: If you want to collaborate on projects, discuss game development, or just chat about gaming and coding, reach out to me at harmeetisop@gmail.com or find me on any of my social platforms.
